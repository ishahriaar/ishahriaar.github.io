Thesis

M M Shahriar Amin, Partho, Jui, Faiza
August 2020
Current Thesis

Machine Learning gained a significant position in healthcare services (HCS) due to its ability to improve the disease prediction in HCS. In present different classification algorithm such as Logistics Regression,Random Forest,Decision Tree,XGBoost shows a significant accuracy to predict the early stage of diabetes and diabetic kidney disease. In this work, we will design a hybrid model that will help to identify the level of diabetes along with Diabetic Kidney Disease (DKD). However, we use different evaluation criteria like ROC to investigate the performance of the model.
